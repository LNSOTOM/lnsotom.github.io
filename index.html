Bio

Laura N. Sotomayor

Geospatial Computer Scientist | Remote Sensing in Ecosystem Research

I work at the intersection of geospatial analysis, optical remote sensing, and ecological mapping, using imagery and machine learning to generate interpretable ecosystem products across complex landscapes.

My current work narrows into arid polar environments, where hyperspectral workflows support analysis of vegetation composition, structure, function, and cross-site transferability.

Research Interests

  • CNN-based ecological mapping in sparse, patchy polar terrain.
  • Fractional vegetation cover composition and structural analysis.
  • Generalisation and transferability across distinct polar sites.
